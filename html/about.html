We are Yale Management Services.



Since 1979, Yale Management Services, Inc. has served owners and tenants across California. We’re a family-owned business dedicated to maximizing our clients’ satisfaction, and providing prompt, customized solutions with a friendly touch.

We specialize in managing commercial buildings and multi-family homes (apartments, condos, townhomes, etc.). Our managers and maintenance team are on-site to serve our tenants and owners whenever they need us, including providing 24/7 emergency service. The best part of our business is meeting new people and adapting our services to fit their needs.

Call us today to schedule an appointment. We’ll work with you step by step to find your new home or create a tailored management plan.

Meet our President Rick Kaufman

Serving as President of Yale Management Services for the past 30 years and counting.

Rick Kaufman

President

Rick Kaufman, president/owner of Yale Management is a predominant real estate broker and investor in the southern California market. Over the past 30 years, Rick has been a part of numerous successful investments and transactions. He brings a depth of experience that is not commonly found in the property management industry.

When Rick initially took helm of the company, Yale Management consisted of roughly 1,000 units and 30 employees. Today, Rick and his dedicated team of more than 150 employees are now managing nearly 4,000 units and consistently growing every day.

With such extensive experience, Rick is confident in his management style, which allows him to make decisions quickly and properly. He has also cultivated a team of dedicated, professional and independent employees who can effortlessly tackle any situation

We currently manage over 5,000 Units.
